Bart Simpson

Bart Simpson is a central character in the animated television series The Simpsons. He is the eldest child of Homer and Marge Simpson, and the brother of Lisa and Maggie. Bart is a mischievous fourth-grade student known for his rebellious behavior and quick wit.

Bart is famous for his pranks, love of skateboarding, and catchphrases like "Eat my shorts!" and "Ay caramba!" He often causes trouble at school and home, but he has a good heart underneath his troublemaking exterior.

Despite his antics, Bart shows loyalty to his family and friends. His playful nature and iconic personality have made him a standout figure in pop culture.

Nancy Cartwright
