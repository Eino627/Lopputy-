Maggie Simpson

Maggie Simpson is the baby of the Simpson family and a recurring character in The Simpsons. Though she rarely speaks, Maggie is known for her pacifier, quiet intelligence, and surprising moments of action.

Despite her age, Maggie has demonstrated remarkable awareness and occasionally takes heroic or comedic actions that surprise those around her.

Her silent charm and hidden cleverness have made Maggie a memorable and unique character in the show's long history.

Nancy Cartwright
