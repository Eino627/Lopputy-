Marge Simpson

Marge Simpson is one of the main characters of the animated television series The Simpsons. She is the mother of Bart, Lisa, and Maggie Simpson, and the wife of Homer Simpson. Marge is a homemaker who often serves as the moral center of the family.

Marge is known for her iconic tall blue hair, patient personality, and strong sense of responsibility. She often finds herself trying to keep her family out of trouble and maintain order in their chaotic lives.

Despite the many challenges she faces, Marge is a loving mother and devoted wife who cares deeply for her family. Her character has become a beloved figure in popular culture.
