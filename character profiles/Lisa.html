Lisa Simpson

Lisa Simpson is the middle child of the Simpson family in the animated series The Simpsons. She is highly intelligent, musically talented, and socially conscious. Lisa is often portrayed as the voice of reason in the family.

Lisa excels at school, plays the saxophone, and is passionate about environmental and social issues. She often struggles to fit in due to her intellect and ideals but remains strong in her convictions.

Her compassion, wisdom, and drive to make the world a better place make Lisa one of the most inspiring and respected characters in the series.

Yeardley Smith
