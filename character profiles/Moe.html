Moe Szyslak

Moe Szyslak is the grumpy yet endearing bartender who runs Moe’s Tavern in The Simpsons. He serves as a friend (and frequent verbal target) of Homer Simpson and other regular patrons like Barney and Lenny.

Moe is known for his rough exterior, raspy voice, and short temper, but he occasionally shows a softer, more vulnerable side. He has a tragicomic life and often struggles with loneliness and failed romances.

Despite his flaws, Moe remains a loyal friend and an irreplaceable part of the Springfield community.

Hank Azaria
