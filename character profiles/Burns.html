Montgomery Burns

Mr. Charles Montgomery Burns is the wealthy and power-hungry owner of the Springfield Nuclear Power Plant in The Simpsons. He is Homer Simpson’s boss and one of the show’s main antagonists.

Mr. Burns is known for his extreme greed, frailty, and catchphrase "Excellent." He often schemes to increase his wealth and power, usually at the expense of the people of Springfield.

Though he is often portrayed as heartless and out of touch, Mr. Burns' exaggerated villainy has made him an iconic character and a source of dark humor throughout the series.
