Apu Nahasapeemapetilon

Apu Nahasapeemapetilon is a recurring character on The Simpsons and the owner of the Kwik-E-Mart convenience store in Springfield. He is known for his dedication to his job, thick accent, and catchphrase, “Thank you, come again!”

Apu is a hardworking immigrant who values family, tradition, and education. He holds a Ph.D. and is deeply devoted to his wife Manjula and their eight children.

Despite the challenges he faces, Apu remains positive and resilient, earning him a place as a beloved and complex character in the series.
